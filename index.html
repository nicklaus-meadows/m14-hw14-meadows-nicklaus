Hobbies

I enjoy playing video games with my brother and a small group of friends on a weekly basis. It is a nice break in our of our hectic lives to just be able to relax and hangout with each other for a while. Our group is still really enjoying Helldivers 2 even after playing it non-stop for months.

Interests

I have always really enjoyed music and movies. Finding a great new song or an amazing show to watch has always been an interest of mine. This past year my wife and I went to a few great concerts. The most recent one was Billie Eilish and she put on a great show.

Career Goals

For my career goals I've always wanted to be a pretty decent web designer. Mostly all my experience up until this class has been self-taught or trial by fire at work. And while I value what I have learned prior to this course it is pretty nice to get a more formal education on the subject matter. So really I would just like to improve my skills and keep doing what I'm doing.

Hobbies:
Other hobbies that I enjoy are working on my old Jeep, gardening, and hiking. The old Jeep that I love to work on is my dad's 98 Jeep Wrangler. It is surprisingly fun to work on, but I feel like most of that comes from the simplicity of the SUV to begin with. You have a lot of room to work and back then they weren't really a luxury car so there isn't a lot of extras to worry about.

I've always loved gardening and landscaping. It has been a good excuse to get outside and do a light workout while having fun and making your house look nice. This year my wife and I got really into growing food in our garden to eventually can. We made some great spaghetti sauce and spicy salsa. We may have went a little overboard because now the pantry has like 25 containers of spaghetti sauce and I'm not sure of the weight limit of the shelves, but we have to be close.

Being outdoors has always been a big part of my life. I really enjoy going out and hiking. We have some pretty decent trails in Ohio surprisingly. Hocking Hills area has some beautiful trails and waterfalls that are great to see and experience between spring and fall. Winter it gets a little sketch, some of the trails are dangerous on a good day, let alone fighting snow and ice. So for the 3 nice seasons in Ohio I try to get outside as much as possible before winter sets in.

Interests:
Other than Billie Eilish this year we have seen Taylor Swift and Stevie Nicks. Both of those were also great shows and great short trips out of town. Next year we hope to see Tyler Childers and Post Malone.

My wife and I also really enjoy finding new movies to watch. We love documentaries and discovering indie films that no one has heard of but end up being a great watch. We recently Heretic and it was pretty good, I always get excited for a good A24 film. Heretic was good, but all considered I at least was expecting a little more, but it was worth seeing.

Career Goals
Currently, I'm an SEO and Web Designer the chemical company I work at. I hope to continue my studies and be able to bring more knowledge and skills to our current environment and hope to one day be able to work into some back-end development as well.
